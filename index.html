Crystals Collector

How to Play :
You will be given a random "Target" number at the start of the game. There are 4 Crystals to choose from. Each Crystal contains a certain value that is added to your score when clicked on. You win the game by matching your number to the "Target" number. Your Wins and Losses will be calculated below. After each game the values of the crystals will be reset. Good Luck!
